Mrs G BRINDA SUNDAR - The Founder & Principal

A post graduate in Science who aspires to contribute in developing a better future generation by shaping the younger minds. She started her journey by coaching students for All India Medical Exams, NTSE, CET, AIEEE and more since 2007.
Her vision to keep children closely connected to nature began with her association to “Kiddies World Play School” in June 2013 which followed the principles of integrated methodology (Montessori, Kindergarten & Nursery).
It was during this phase that she felt the necessity for change in the education system and started a Montessori school which will unwrap the world to the children; exposing them to its flawless beauty, wonders and promises, helping them to become all of who they want to be and a better human being.
She equipped herself as a certified Montessori primary practitioner for the age group of 3-6 from the International Montessori Society in the year 2015, elementary practitioner for the age group of 6-12 years from Indian Montessori center in the year 2017 and pursued infancy and parenting program under Mrs. Vidhya Shankar, in the year 2019.
